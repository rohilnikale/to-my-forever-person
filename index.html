My Love ❤️

Happy 3 Months, cutie. ❤️ Three months… sounds small when you say it, but for me these three months have given me so many memories that I genuinely don’t want to forget. We’ve laughed, irritated each other, had cute moments, had stupid fights, misunderstandings, mood swings… basically everything. 😂 But somehow, after all of that, I still choose you every single day, and I always will. I know I’m not a perfect boyfriend. There have been times when my tone wasn’t right, when I reacted without thinking, or when I made small things bigger than they needed to be. I’m really sorry for all of that. You never deserved those moments, and I hate knowing that I was the reason you felt hurt. I can’t promise that we’ll never fight again because that’s not how relationships work. But I can promise that I’ll keep trying to be better. I’ll watch my tone, control my behaviour, understand you before reacting, and never stop putting effort into us. At the end of every argument, every misunderstanding, every rough day… I still want it to be you. Tari sathe hoy tyare badhu thodu easy lage che. ❤️ Mane khabar che apda vachche ups and downs avse, pan hu promise karu chu ke hu kyare pan apde ne give up nathi karvano. Hu hamesha tari side par rahish, tane samajva ni ane tane khush rakhva ni puri koshish karish. Bas aatlu yaad rakhje… mara mate tu hamesha special hati, che ane rahish. Thank you for these beautiful three months. I hope one day we’ll read these messages together and laugh about all our silly fights, because we’ll have made it through all of them. I love you so much, Palak. Happy 3 Months to us. ❤️
